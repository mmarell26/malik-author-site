About Malik

Malik R. Marell is a father, veteran, real estate investor, and builder of men. He has survived trauma, chaos, and heartbreak – and transformed that pain into a blueprint for purpose, healing, and legacy.

Through his writing, Malik doesn’t pretend to be perfect. He writes from the middle of the fight – from custody battles, family responsibilities, business pressure, and spiritual warfare – with one clear message: you can rebuild your life from nothing and become unbreakable.

When he’s not writing, Malik is building Perfection Real Estate Investors, Marell Dynasty Group, and creating housing solutions for families and veterans. His work is rooted in fatherhood, faith, and a deep commitment to breaking generational cycles.

This author site is your home for Malik’s books, live events, and new projects – all anchored in one mission: helping people rise from what tried to destroy them.

The Book: UNBREAKABLE: A MAN REBORN

What This Book Is

UNBREAKABLE: A MAN REBORN is not a “feel good” story. It’s a resurrection story – written in raw, poetic language that walks through:

  • Childhood pain and family trauma
  • Broken relationships and betrayal
  • PTSD, anxiety, and silent battles
  • Fatherhood, courtrooms, and custody fights
  • Rebuilding as a man, leader, and protector

Who It’s For

This book is for men and women who have been through hell and refuse to stay broken. It’s for:

  • Fathers fighting for their kids
  • Mothers carrying more than anyone knows
  • People healing from trauma, loss, or betrayal
  • Anyone rebuilding their life from the ground up

If you’ve ever felt like your story was over, this book reminds you it’s just getting started.
